ABOUT US

Every city has a character. Every place has a character. What`s interesting though, is that food can bring the flavours of other places right where you are. Bringing cuisines that respect time from all over the world, Barometer aims to give you an experience, not just food. We celebrate the time that gets involved in making delicious food, it is the slowness that drives us, the care taken in preparation without rushing or hurring is what we specialize in, come experience the pause at that first bite yours at Barometer.

The idea is simple to provide great food and friendly service to each customer we serve. No matter where you come from you can expect friendly service and same friendly restaurant atmosphere, great service and delicious food to look forward to, any day of the week. Come, indulge!

BRING BAROMETER WHEREVER YOU ARE

At Barometer, nothing gives us more pleasure than seeing a large group of people seated at tables that had to be joined to fit their quorum - with our food doing the rounds amidst endlessly flowing talks, banter, discussions and drinks. We thought we could work around this hurdle by bringing Barometer wherever you are.
